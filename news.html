Interview with Paul Ayris

07.04.2011

A major Estonian daily newspaper /Postimees/ published an interview with LIBER President Paul Ayris about the role of libraries in the modern world and about the activities of the LIBER on 25 January 2011. Text of the interview in Estonian

LIBER Board in Tartu

07.04.2011

On 19-21 January 2011, the LIBER Board members visited the University of Tartu Library, holding the Board meeting and signing the agreement about the organisation of the LIBER Annual Conference in Tartu in 2012. The Board members visited the future venue of the Conference and saw the sights of Tartu.

LIBER Groupe des Cartothécaires

07.04.2011

The 17th Conference of the LIBER Groupe des Cartothécaires was held in 15-18 June 2010 in Tallinn with one conference day spent in the university town of Tartu. The general theme of the conference was "A Map Library as the Centre of Cartographic Informatione".The organizers - the National Library of Estonia and the University of Tartu Library.
